General Announcement

FanCafè, January 7, 2023

Happy New Year everyone!

In the previous months we have organized our Fanclub activity to reflect your needs and requests. Here's a MAJOR update:

  • You will find in the calendar weekly missions that we have shared with our admins. The following are:
  • Weekly post on Monday, so that we can keep this site alive:
  • Every two weeks you'll find the FanCafè, which is our in person meeting at the [redacted] Cafè. You'll be admitted only if you're a premium member.
  • Every two weeks you'll find the Fanclub Activities, which is a list of all news, events plans and updates on prvious meetings;
  • Every last Thurday of the Month we'll organize a Monthly Budget Meeting, where we'll talk with our admins and bursar on the balance that we have spent (more this than others), the gain though projects and what we can afford to do for the rest of the fans;
  • Every three Fridays you'll find a Q & A session, where we'll listen to your questions and resolve some doubts. REMINDER: you can partecipate by adding the event on your calendar, but you'll be only admitted to speak if you are a premium member. If not, your webcam and microphone will be deactivated.

That is all for our announcements, hope everything is clear!
If not please contact us on: support@unofficial-fanclub-party-phil.com.
Thank you!
