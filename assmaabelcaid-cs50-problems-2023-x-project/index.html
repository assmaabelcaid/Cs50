What's Wii Party


Wii Party is a party game developed by NDcube and Nintendo and published by Nintendo in 2010 for the Wii. It is a game that features gameplay that often needs more than two players. It's a family and friends game in the Wii Series (Wii Sports, Wii Play, Wii Music)

Conquer the world with Globe Trot!

Players have to travel around the World to visit Hot Spots and collect souvenir photos.
The player who has the most at the end of the game wins.

Test your compatibility with a Friend!

Friend Connection is a Pair Game mode for 2 players in Wii Party. In this game, you have to answer five questions and playa Pair Minigame (ranked A, B, C or D) to test if the players are the best or the worst pair.

Play minigames and win first place!

Wii party offers a total of 87 games. Play against other 3 Player, 1 vs 3 , 1 vs 1, pair and Challenge Minigames.

Who's Party Phil?


Read some amazing facts about the true star of Wii Party!

Party Phil in the game

Party Phil, known as Woopy in the Japanese version of the first Wii Party, is a Tute. He is the host of Wii Party and the co-host of Wii Party U, alongside his younger sister: Party Penny. The player will first encounter Phil when they first start the game for the first time. He will introduce the player to the game.

Suggestions

At the start of every game mode, Phil will ask you if you want to watch the tutorial on how to play the game mode and if you say yes, Party Phil will begin explaining that mode's rules. He also constantly appears throughout Wii Party's various game modes providing commentary on what is happening or going to happen. For example, in Board Game Island, Party Phil Picks what the next minigame will be until later on when he instead chooses 3 minigames for the last place Mii to pick from. There is also a Suggestions button where Party Phil will give suggest game modes to the player/s based on how many people are playing and how long they intend to play for.

Wii U Saga

In Wii Party U, Party Phil's role is the same as Wii Party. However, this time we are introduced to Party Phil's younger sister: Party Penny. Party Penny liberates Party Phil from his hosting duties for the game's minigame-centric modes.
